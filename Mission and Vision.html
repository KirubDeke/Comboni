Vision and Mission

Vision

CSPS strives to be a model of excellence in education fostering the integral growth of young people through discipline and knowledge, and offering to the society humble and mature citizens who may take an active part in the development of the nation.

Mission

The mission of CSPS is:


  • Modeling God-fearing citizens
  • Providing quality learning experiences in classroom and in real life encounters
  • Focusing on discipline, knowledge and wisdom

Always more always better for God and our Country
