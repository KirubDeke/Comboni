Promotion Policy

Promotion Policy

  1. Students with less than a yearly-average of 50% in 1-3 subjects will have to sit for re-exam on those subjects at the end of August of the same year.
  2. Students who fail to pass one or more re-exams will be detained. The re-admission to the school will be discretional.
  3. Students with less than a yearly-average of 50% in 4-4 subjects will be detained or dismissed, according to overall behaviour and at the discretion of the school.
  4. Students with less than a yearly-average of 50% in 6 and more subjects will automatically be dismissed on acadamic grounds.
