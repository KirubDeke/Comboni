WELCOME TO SAINT DANIEL COMBONI SECONDARY AND PREPARATORY SCHOOL HAWASSA

The Comboni Catholic High School was established by the Comboni Missionaries, a Catholic religious inistitute founded by St. Daniel Comboni in 1987. The school was established in 1998 and since become one of the leading high school in Hawassa, providing education to both boys and girls. The school is named after St. Daniel Comboni in honour of his missionary work in Africa.
