Reporting

  1. Student's progress is monitored throughout the school year. Parents/guardians are called to check the students' progress four times a year: first semester mid-term, end of the second semester.
  2. Dates for grade reporting are fixed at the begining of the year and published in the schoool calendar. Any change will be communicated in written form to parents/guardians.
  3. A student whose parents/guardians fail to come and check students' progress will be dismissed.
  4. Generally grades may not be changed on report cards after cards have been issued, unless there has been an error in calculating the grade.
  5. All unresolved grade disputes must be promptly reported by the student and the parent to the school administration.
