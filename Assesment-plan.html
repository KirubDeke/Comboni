Assesment Plan

  1. The scchool follows a policy of continious assesment of the students' learning abilities.
  2. Tests are given on a regular basis according to the school calandar in all subjects.
  3. The result of tests, homework, assignments, and of any other project is recorded and accounts for 50% of the final mark. Semester examinations account for 50% of the final mark. An average of 50% for each subject (semester one and two divided by two) is the annual passing mark.
